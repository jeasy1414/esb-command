Privacy Policy — Easy Street Brain

Effective date: July 21, 2026

Easy Street Brain is an internal business analytics application owned and operated by Easy Street Buyers LLC ("we," "us"). It is used solely by Easy Street Buyers LLC to analyze the company's own business data, including accounting data accessed from the company's own QuickBooks Online account via the Intuit API.

What data we access

With authorization from the company's QuickBooks administrator, the application accesses Easy Street Buyers LLC's own accounting records (such as profit & loss reports, transactions, invoices, and vendor/customer records) through the QuickBooks Online API.

How data is used

Accessed data is used exclusively for internal financial reporting and business analysis for Easy Street Buyers LLC. It is not sold, rented, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.

Data storage and security

Data is stored in access-controlled systems operated for Easy Street Buyers LLC. API credentials and tokens are stored securely and are accessible only to authorized company personnel.

Third parties

We do not disclose QuickBooks data to third parties except as required by law.

Data retention and deletion

Data is retained only as long as needed for internal business analysis. To request deletion or disconnection, contact us at the address below; disconnecting the app from QuickBooks revokes its access immediately.
